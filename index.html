Humanities @ IIT

Minors in Public Policy, Policy & Ethics, and Architecture

I’m a 4th-year student passionate about the intersections of policy, ethics, and the built environment. My work bridges human narratives with systemic structures, and I find inspiration in both nature and the city.

Artwork

I use digital iconography, recordings of ambient noise, randomized drum beats, and samples from my favorite songs and movies to represent the search for peace in a capitalist society.

Academic

Research in urban rewilding, community engagement, and legal personhood for ecosystems.

Interests

Sketching, meandering, listening to music, watching baseball, and philosophizing.
